## Show HN: Groundhoge Day — A Solana token where supply is determined by a groundhog

**URL:** https://groundhogeday.com

**Body (keep short for HN):**

Groundhoge Day ($HOGE) is a Solana token with weather-dependent deflationary mechanics. Every February 2nd, the real Groundhog Day ceremony determines whether the supply burns or expands:

- Shadow → 6% of total supply burned on-chain
- No shadow → 3.9% minted to all holders
- Every number in the tokenomics traces back to real Groundhog Day lore (1887 founding, 39% accuracy, 6 weeks of winter, etc.)

The site is styled like a Bloomberg terminal. We went deep on the lore — full historical database back to 1887, classified dossiers on Phil's biology and rival groundhogs, shareable "prophecy dispatches," and an interactive terminal with live on-chain swap functionality.

Built with Next.js, deployed on Vercel, programs on Solana devnet. The ceremony trigger is an on-chain multisig transaction.

The absurdist-corporate aesthetic was intentional — serious fintech presentation, absurd content. We wanted it to feel like a Goldman Sachs research note about a rodent.
